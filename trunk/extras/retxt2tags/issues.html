Limitations and known issues

Disclamer: As the reTxt2tags tool is derived from reMarkdown CSS, most of the details here are no longer relevant.

ReTxt2tags was tested on recent versions of modern browsers. It works well in all browsers with complete CSS 2.1 support, including IE8. It will degrade not-so-gracefully in IE7.

  • The tables may not display that nice.
  • You can't select the generated elements just like normal text. But you can export to pdf and then to plain text.

Browser-specific issues

  • IE8 does not render generated content in HR elements. Your horizontal rules will be invisible in that browser.
