Notes

  • These are free tools to improve your user experience. They are optional, not required to run txt2tags.

  • In links that point to the tool code, it's best to choose "Save as" instead making a copy & paste.

  • Some tools were made by the txt2tags author, some by kind users.

  • Wanna contribute? Just send your work (or a link) to the author at <verde (@) aurelio net> and you will be part of our team.
